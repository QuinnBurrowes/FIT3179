Physical, Mental, and Lifestyle Health in Australia

A visual exploration of health outcomes, behaviours, and lifestyle differences across Australia. Data: ABS National Health Survey (2022) & AIHW.

Geographic Health Patterns in Australia

Map A — Prevalence of mental health conditions by state.
Map B — Proportion meeting physical activity guidelines by state.
These maps show significant state-level variation in wellbeing. Mental health conditions appear more common in some northern regions, while physical activity compliance is generally stronger in southern states. This highlights how environment and accessibility shape population health.

Lifestyle and Health Behaviours by Age Group

The heatmap illustrates how fruit and vegetable consumption improves with age, while the donut chart shows that younger Australians are less likely to achieve recommended physical activity levels. These behaviours contribute to early onset chronic conditions and differences in wellbeing.
